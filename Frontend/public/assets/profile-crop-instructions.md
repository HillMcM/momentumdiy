# Profile Image Cropping Instructions

## For the "Why I Built MomentumDIY" Section

Based on the image description, here's how to crop the profile image:

### Target Crop Area:
- **Focus**: Face and upper body of the person sitting on the steps
- **Frame**: From just above the head to mid-torso (around waist level)
- **Background**: Include some of the weathered white door frame for context
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:5 or 3:4 (portrait orientation)

### Crop Coordinates (approximate):
- **Top**: Start just above the head (leave some breathing room)
- **Bottom**: End at mid-torso (around where the blazer ends)
- **Left**: Include part of the door frame on the left
- **Right**: Include part of the brick wall on the right

### Final Image Specs:
- **Size**: 400x500px or 300x400px
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Quality**: High resolution for web use
- **File Name**: `profile-founder.jpg`

### Tools to Use:
1. **Online**: Canva, Photopea (free Photoshop alternative)
2. **Desktop**: Preview (Mac), GIMP (free), Photoshop
3. **Mobile**: Built-in photo editing apps

### Upload Instructions:
Once cropped, save the image as `profile-founder.jpg` in the `Frontend/public/assets/` folder.
