## Business Model Correction

### **MomentumDIY - Marketing Clarity Platform**
- **Target Market**: Small business owners (cafes, home services, personal services, brick-and-mortar shops) who are overwhelmed by marketing options and need clarity
- **Core Problem**: Marketing overwhelm and lack of focus - too many options, scattered efforts, not knowing where to start

- **Platform Features**: Kanban task tracker, email campaign management, Meta Business Suite integration, 24/7 AI marketing assistant
